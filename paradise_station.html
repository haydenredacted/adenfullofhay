Paradise Station

what even IS a paradise station?

paradise station is a space station 13 server that i enjoy playing on A LOT. i have put an unspeakable amount of hours into this server in the past few years, even helped contribute to it and are actively on its development team!



space station 13, an open source roleplaying game developed using the BYOND system which was originally released in 2003, has you playing the role of someone working on a space station where anything from vampires attacking you, radioactive anamolous crystals exploding, and a clown slipping you can happen!
